Vasool Management

Features built for real collections & finance work

From daily collection lines to loans, expenses, partners, and exports. Everything stays connected so your team sees one clear picture.

Collection management

  • Create and manage unlimited daily, weekly, and monthly collection lines
  • Add areas and customers with photos and documents
  • Clear view of paid and pending amounts

Loans & payments

  • Add and manage loans with ease
  • Track EMI payments and due schedules
  • Access complete payment history anytime

Financial tracking

  • Monitor daily collections and pending EMIs
  • Record and manage daily expenses
  • Track investments efficiently
  • Clear insights into cash flow

Partner management

  • Add and manage business partners or agents
  • Role-based permissions for each user
  • Control access based on responsibilities

Reports & insights

  • Daily collection summaries
  • Customer, loan, and payment reports
  • Expense and investment reports
  • Advanced reports: Plan, Ledger, and Multiline
  • Export or share reports easily

Security & privacy

  • Secure login with password protection
  • Fingerprint and face unlock support
  • Built to keep your business data protected
